## Features That Actually Get Used

Let's skip the marketing fluff and focus on what people actually use daily:

**Global Virtual Numbers**: Get local, mobile, toll-free, or national numbers from 100+ countries. Your startup in Singapore can have a London number for UK clients, a New York number for US prospects, and a Dubai number for Middle East partners. Instant setup. No physical presence required.

**Power Dialer**: For outbound teams, this accelerates calling by automatically dialing the next number when you finish a call. Voicemail drops save time when no one answers. Connect-rate tracking shows which times and approaches work best. Sales teams report doubling their daily outreach capacity.

**Multi-Level IVR (Phone Trees)**: Route callers to the right department automatically. "Press 1 for sales, press 2 for support." Sounds dated, but when you're handling volume, professional call routing prevents frustrated customers and wasted time.

**AI Copilot Features**: The AI summarizes calls, creates transcripts, generates action items, and suggests quick replies for SMS. This isn't revolutionary AIâ€”it's practical automation that saves 10-15 minutes after each important call. For managers reviewing team performance, searchable call transcripts beat listening to 45-minute recordings.

**Bulk SMS Campaigns**: Send mass texts for promotions, appointment reminders, or updates. Combined with AI-powered rephrasing, your messaging stays professional across hundreds of contacts. Real estate agents, healthcare practices, and service businesses use this constantly.

**Call Monitoring & Coaching**: Managers can listen to live calls (Monitor), speak to agents without customers hearing (Whisper), or join conversations (Barge). For contact centers and sales teams, this transforms training from quarterly reviews to real-time improvement.

**100+ CRM Integrations**: Native connections to HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zendesk, Freshsales, Zoho, and more. Click-to-call from contact records. Automatic call logging. Conversation histories sync both ways. No manual data entry.

**Business Hours & Failover Routing**: Calls outside business hours go to voicemail or forward to mobile. If your primary number fails, calls route to backup numbers. Simple reliability features that prevent lost opportunities.
